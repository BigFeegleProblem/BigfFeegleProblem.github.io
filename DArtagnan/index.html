D'Artagnan

Paladin - Level 1

Little is known of D'Artagnan's youth, except that he lived within the walls of the city of Minas Tirith with his sister Estelle. He spent most of his childhood hiding himself and his sister from the forces of darkness, who sought to destroy them. One day, Estelle mysteriously disappeared, and D'Artagnan left the walls of the city to search for her.
