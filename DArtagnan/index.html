D'Artagnan

Paladin - Level 1

Little is known of D'Artagnan's youth, except that he lived within the walls of the city of Minas Tirith with his sister Estelle. He spent most of his childhood hiding himself and his sister from the forces of darkness, who sought to destroy them. One day, Estelle mysteriously disappeared, and D'Artagnan left the walls of the city to search for her.

A dark apparition has been haunting D'Artagnan's family for generations now, foreshadowing unfortunate events and bad luck. While D'Artagnan was growing up, he only saw the sign once or twice, and marked it down as a hallucination. However, it appeared practically everywhere he looked just before Estelle disappeared, and since then it has come back to haunt him more and more often. It's getting to the point now where barely two days can go by without him seeing it.
