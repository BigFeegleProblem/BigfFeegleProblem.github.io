Wilhelm

Warlock - Level 1

Wilhelm was a fisherman from Gondor who was sailing on the Belegaer one day when his boat was caught in a storm. His ship was destroyed, and the rest of the crew was drowned. Wilhelm would have drowned too, but the legendary Kraken saw that he had a predisposition to magical power and saved him in return for his services in the future.
